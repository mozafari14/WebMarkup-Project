A place where Asian immigrants reconnect through food and culture

Moving to a new country can be both exciting and challenging. For many Asian residents in Rotterdam, food becomes a bridge between memories of home and new experiences in the city.

Asia Meets Rotterdam helps you find authentic flavors, meet local food lovers, and celebrate the traditions that bring comfort and connection.

On this website, you can:

  • Find local Asian-owned restaurants and shops.
  • Explore recipes shared by the Rotterdam Asian community.
  • Read stories from people about how food connects cultures.
  • Join community events and exchange your own experiences.
